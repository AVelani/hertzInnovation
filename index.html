About Us

Our expertise empowers industries with advanced digital solutions, enhancing operational efficiency, sustainability, and reliability in energy and automation systems.

We Are HertzInnovation

Hertz Innovation is a cutting-edge technology company specializing in industrial automation, energy automation, smart grid solutions, and renewable energy system integration. We provide innovative and customized solutions for the power sector, ensuring seamless automation, real-time monitoring, and efficient energy management.

Established with a vision to drive digital transformation in the energy sector, Hertz Innovation delivers state-of-the-art automation and IoT solutions for solar, wind, and battery energy storage systems (BESS). Our expertise spans across SCADA systems, telemetry solutions, industrial IoT, and weather monitoring technologies.
